types of selectors

1)universal selector (*) 
     2)element selector (element_name)
     3)class selector (.class_name)
     4)id selector (#)
     5)attribute selector (element_name[attribute_name]) or (element_name[attribute_name="value_name"])
     6)group selector (group of element)
     7)descendant selector (parent child)
     8)child selector (.class_name or id_name>child)
     9)adjacent sibling selector (parent + first_child)
     10)general sibling selector (parent ~ all_childs)
     11)pseudo element selectors
     12)pseudo class selector
