Prabhas

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju ([pɾabʱaːs]; born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu cinema.[4] He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015.[9] Referred to in the media as Rebel Star, he has appeared in over 20 films, and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award.[10][11]

vijay devarakonda

Deverakonda Vijay Sai(born 9 May 1989), widely known as Vijay Deverakonda,[1] is an Indian actor and film producer who works in Telugu films.[2][3] One of the highest-paid actors of Telugu cinema, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 since 2018. Vijay is an recipient of a Filmfare Award, a Nandi Award, three SIIMA Awards and a Gaddar Telangana Film Awards.[4]
